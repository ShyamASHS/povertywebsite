About us

A bit about us!

As part of the ASHS community, we want to come together and try to overcome the issue of Poverty. We want to highlight the root cause of poverty around the world and inform you about the situation. There are many ways you can help to stop this issue. It can be as little as telling your friends about poverty and sharing them something you learnt from this website, or as big as donating money that goes towards people in need!
